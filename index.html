California Counties

San Bernardino: Likely

  • The Last fire was a structural fire at a house that killed one firefighter and injured several others.
  • In this area you are most likely to encounter a structural fire(i.e. houses, playgrounds,buildings)
  • Since fires have been spotted recently but are infrequent, you are likely to encounter a fire.

Inyo: Not Likely

  • The Last fire was August 1st 2018, the fire was visible from Highway 395 and they are asking people to avoid the areas so firefighters can respond safely.
  • In this area you are more likely to encounter wild fires, including brush fires and forest fires.
  • Since Inyo's last fire was last year and the fires are infrequent, you are not likely to encounter a fire.

Trinity: Highly Likely

  • The Last fire was September 4th 2018, the fire took over 1751 acres and was caused by Wildfire. Still the cause of the wildfire is unknown.
  • >In this area you are more likely to encounter wild fires, including brush fires and forest fires.
  • Since fires in Trinity are frequent and the last years cause is unknown(which means we cant prevent it from happening again) it is Highly Likely that you will encounter a fire.
